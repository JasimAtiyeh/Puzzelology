Welcome to Puzzleology!

A classic puzzle game to kill time and boredom.

Select your difficulty, select an image, and solve the puzzle.

Choosing a difficulty will add pieces to the puzzle.

You can choose from a variety of stock images or you can upload your owm image.

Use your mouse to drag and drop the pieces into place.
